# May 2026 Update

For this month's update, we've incorporated the May security patch from `android-security-16.0.0_r6` into Android 16 QPR2. Alongside that, we've also included a set of improvements and fixes focused on usability, polish, and everyday quality-of-life enhancements.

<Banner src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/PixelOS-CI/blog_assets/refs/heads/main/2026-05-09/banner.webp" />

As always, detailed changelogs can be found on our [changelogs page](https://blog.pixelos.net/changelogs).

## Misc Changes
- Lockscreen Widgets have been added, allowing quick access to useful controls and information directly from the lockscreen.
- Smartspace has been updated to CP1A.260305.018.
