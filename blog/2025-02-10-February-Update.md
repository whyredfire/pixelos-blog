# February 2025 Update

Lots of new stuff this month!

We've a new infrastructure for serving OTA updates, much faster and reliable. Older builds will still be archived to our sourceforge as usual. Thanks to h@x.

New blog website; modern, cleaner and faster than former.

Full changelogs can now be viewed at https://blog.pixelos.net/changelogs/, and is auto updated daily ;)

Translations for PixelOS 15 has been merged with this update, and will be updated monthly. Kudos to the contributors at https://translate.pixelos.net for bringing PixelOS to the community in their native languages.

This update includes the latest security patches (from `android-15.0.0_r17`).

<Banner src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/PixelOS-CI/blog_assets/refs/main/2025-02-10/banner.png">

## Changelogs

    - Updated themed icons
    - Fixed an issue where updater would download official updates from Google servers
    - Added back tap (on supported devices)
    - Fixed an issue where volume panel would float on the screen on devices with volume buttons on the left
    - Added support for per-app volume control
    - Added a quick settings tile for adjusting flashlight strength (on supported devices)
    - Under the hood changes and improvements
